2.  万能句式
①  It is + adj. + for sb. + to do sth.
e.g.  It  is  imperative  for  policymakers to  integrate  environmental  protection  into  urban  planning, as  sustainable
development hinges on this crucial balance.{桔二六}
政策制定者必须将环境保护纳入城市规划，因为可持续发展正依赖于这种关键的平衡。

②  ... so ... that ... / ... such a/an ... that ... (如此……以至于……)

e.g.  The  pace  of  technological  innovation  is so  rapid  that it  constantly  redefines  the  skills  required  in  the  job
market, rendering lifelong learning no longer an option but a necessity.
技术创新的步伐如此之快，以至于它不断重新定义就业市场所需的技能，使得终身学习不再是一种选择，而

③  not only ... but also ... (不仅……而且……)  【倒装更高级】
e.g. Not only does cultural exchange foster mutual understanding between nations, but it also inspires innovation by
providing fresh perspectives that challenge conventional thinking.
文化交流不仅促进国家间的相互理解，更能通过提供挑战传统思维的新视角来激发创新。

④  The reason why ... is that ... (……的原因是……)
e.g. The primary reason why the digital divide remains a pressing issue is that unequal access to technology is
exacerbating social inequality, limiting opportunities for education and employment in underprivileged communities.
{桔二六}
数字鸿沟依然是一个紧迫问题，其主要原因在于技术获取的不平等正在加剧社会不公，限制了贫困社区的
教育和就业机会。

⑤  Not until... do/have...（直到……才……）
e.g. Not until we step out of our cultural comfort zones do we truly begin to appreciate the complexity and beauty
of global diversity.
直到我们走出文化的舒适区，才能真正开始欣赏全球多样性的复杂与美妙。

⑥  It is/was +  被强调部分  + that/who +  句子其余部分  【强调句】
e.g.  It  is the  relentless  pursuit  of  short-term  profits that often  leads  corporations  to  neglect  their  long-term
environmental responsibilities.
正是对短期利润的无休止追求，才常常导致企业忽视其长期的环境责任。

⑦  Were it not for / Had it not been for... （如果不是因为…）【虚拟语气】
e.g. Were it not for the internet, the global collaboration that led to the rapid development of vaccines would have
been unimaginable.
若不是因为互联网，导致疫苗快速研发的全球协作将是无法想象的。

⑧  It is high time that sb. did sth. （早就该做某事了）{桔二六}
e.g.  It  is high  time  that educational  policymakers placed equal emphasis  on  students'  mental  resilience  and  their
academic performance.
教育政策制定者早就该将学生的心理韧性与学业表现置于同等重要的位置。

⑨  What lies at the heart of the issue is... （问题的核心是…）
e.g. What lies at the heart of the digital divide is not merely access to devices, but the disparity in the quality of
access and digital literacy.

三、结尾段
1. In conclusion, ... / To sum up, ... / In a nutshell, ... (总之，……)
【只要开始写结尾，上来就写这几个词】
2. From what has been discussed above, we can safely come to the conclusion that ...
综上所述，我们可以得出结论……
3. Only in this way can we ... (只有这样，我们才能……)  【倒装句】
e.g. Only in this way can we cultivate a generation of resilient, empathetic, and critical thinkers who are equipped
to navigate the complexities of the 21st century.
（只有这样，我们才能培养出一代坚韧、富有同理心和批判精神的思考者，他们有能力应对 21 世纪的复杂
挑战。）
e.g.  Only  by  embracing  both  traditional  knowledge  and  innovative  thinking can  we find  sustainable  solutions  to
environmental challenges.
（只有同时接纳传统智慧和创新思维，我们才能找到应对环境挑战的可持续方案。）
